School Website for Kaiserslautern High School

Due to a dislike of the official DODEA school website, KHS had a second website made in the Web Design class. However, due to security complaints of Google Sites as well as the lack of effort in the design of the website, the KHS Google Site was phased out. But, people were still discontent with the DODEA school site, so I took it upon myself to make a new school website that was more pleasing to look at than the DODEA site, as well as better designed and more secure than the Google Site. So, using Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS, I made the new school website. It is currently at KaiserslauternHS.rocks, utilizing a free domain from the GitHub Student Developer Pack, and is hosted on GitHub as well.
